Kang Pride

We are Code Purple (Team 5827), an FRC robotics club from Lake Washington High School and local magnet academies, based in Kirkland, Washington. The club was started in 2011 as a VRC team. For three years, exceptional STEM students have gotten our robots to the Worlds competition and won numerous awards. For the 2016 season, the team decided to switch from the Vex to FRC for a greater challenge and more opportunities to enhance STEM principles.
