Steamworks

The 2017 FRC Game




Game Overview

FIRST Steamworks, stylized as FIRST STEAMworks, was the FIRST Robotics Competition game for the 2017 season. As in past games, two alliances of three individual teams and their robots compete on a field to score "match" point to win the game and ranking points to advance to playoff rounds. The game has a steampunk theme and teams are required to shoot wiffle balls which represent fuel into a simulated boiler which transfers the generated steam into an airship in the middle of the field. Each alliance has one airship, which they pressurize with steam from the boiler and load with plastic gears from the field. At the end of the match, robots can climb and hang on team-supplied ropes (or standard ropes supplied by FIRST) attached to the airship for additional points. Wikipedia


Our Bot

Our bot was made with a 6x6 drive train in a minimalistic fashion. The frame was made lightweight, allowing to fly at speeds up to 18-20 feet per second. Combined with an effective gear collection & drop off system, our bot scored many points very quick. Our climber, made to climb ropes was very effective, climbing 8 feet in a mere second, which lead us to win engineering awards
